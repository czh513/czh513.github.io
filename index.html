Short Summary
  • I am currently a research-intensive Lecturer (equivalent to Assistant Professor in North American universities) in Computer Science at the School of ICT, University of Tasmania (UTas). I am the recipient of ARC Discovery Early Career Researcher Award (DECRA) Fellowship 2022. Before joining UTas, I was working at University of Technology Sydney (UTS), and received my PhD degree from Australian AI Institute in 2017 at UTS, under the supervision of Distinguished Prof. Chin-Teng Lin.

  • My research achievements build the bridge between human cognition and artificial intelligence and its applications to support decision making. My most significant research contribution to date has been to spearhead a breakthrough in "computational intelligence for brain-computer interface" and "human-in-the-loop reinforcement learning". My current research primarily focuses on developing a closed-loop human–agent learning framework to enhance decision making. If you are a self-motivated student and keen on a relevant research project to work with me, please drop me an email.
